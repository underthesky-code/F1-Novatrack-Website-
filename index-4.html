Our Mission

RACING REDEFINED

NovaTrack exists to push the boundaries of what students can achieve in motorsport engineering. We're not here to participate—we're here to dominate.

Our mission is simple: design, build, and race the fastest miniature F1 car ever created by a student team. We combine cutting-edge technology with relentless determination.

"No Margins. Just Wins." isn't just a slogan—it's our philosophy. Every gram, every millisecond, every decision is optimized for victory.
